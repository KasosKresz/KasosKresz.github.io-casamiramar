Request A Direct Stay At SierraMar

Send your dates and guest details through the form below. Availability is checked manually against current bookings, and once your stay is confirmed you will receive a Stripe payment link or invoice.

1. Send your request

Enter your travel dates, guest count, and contact details. You can also include a coupon code or any special request.

2. Availability is confirmed

Your requested stay is checked manually before payment is requested. If the dates are not available, alternative dates can be discussed.

3. Pay by Stripe

After confirmation, a Stripe payment link or Stripe invoice is sent manually. Your booking is confirmed once payment is received.
