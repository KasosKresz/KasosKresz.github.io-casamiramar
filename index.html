Holiday home in Salobreña, Costa Tropical

Casa Miramar is a holiday home in Salobreña, Granada, in the Costa Tropical of Andalusia, Spain. If you are looking for an old town stay in southern Spain with sea views, beach access, and easy walks to the castle, restaurants, and mirador, Casa Miramar is a comfortable base for exploring this beautiful part of Andalusia.

Comfortable accommodations for families and small groups

Casa Miramar Salobreña offers three bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a bright open-concept living room with a sofa bed and dining area. It is a welcoming base for up to 8 guests looking to enjoy the charm of Salobreña’s old town in the Costa Tropical of Andalusia, southern Spain.

The home combines traditional character with practical comfort, making it ideal for families, couples traveling together, or small groups who want space, convenience, and a lovely terrace to relax in.

Stay in the heart of Salobreña, Andalusia

Casa Miramar is located in Salobreña’s picturesque old town on a peaceful pedestrian street near the Castle. Guests can easily explore the Mirador, restaurants, beaches, and the relaxed coastal atmosphere of the Costa Tropical in southern Spain.

Playa de Salobreña is less than 0.6 miles away, the Balcón de Europa is 22 miles away, and Federico García Lorca Granada-Jaén Airport is about 47 miles from the property.
