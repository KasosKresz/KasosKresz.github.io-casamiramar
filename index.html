Comfortable accommodations for families and small groups

Casa Miramar Salobreña offers three bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a bright open-concept living room with a sofa bed and dining area. It is a welcoming base for up to 8 guests looking to enjoy the charm of Salobreña’s old town and the Costa Tropical.

The home combines traditional character with practical comfort, making it ideal for families, couples traveling together, or small groups who want space, convenience, and a lovely terrace to relax in.

Stay in the heart of Salobreña

Casa Miramar is located in Salobreña’s picturesque old town on a peaceful pedestrian street near the Castle. Guests can easily explore the Mirador, restaurants, beaches, and the relaxed coastal atmosphere of the Costa Tropical.

Playa de Salobreña is less than 0.6 miles away, the Balcón de Europa is 22 miles away, and Federico García Lorca Granada-Jaén Airport is about 47 miles from the property.
