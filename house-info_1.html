How To Play Music Through The House Speakers

  1. Turn on the Logitech Bluetooth device.
  2. Open Bluetooth settings on your phone, tablet, or laptop.
  3. Connect to the Logitech device.
  4. Once connected, your music will play through the house speakers.
  5. Use the volume controls on your device and on the speakers if needed.

If the connection does not start right away, turn Bluetooth off and on again and try reconnecting.

How To Use The BenQ Projector

  1. Turn on the projector with the remote or the power button.
  2. Wait for the projector to fully start.
  3. Use the cable provided at the house to connect a laptop with a USB-C connector to the projector.
  4. Guests should bring their own laptop with a USB-C connector if they want to use the projector.
  5. Select the input source that matches the connected laptop cable, then adjust focus and volume if needed.

Important: The cable available in the house is for connecting a laptop with a USB-C connector to the projector.

Please leave the cable and remote near the projector after use.

How To Use The Air Conditioning

  1. Use the air-conditioning remote fixed on the wall.
  2. Press the power button to turn the unit on or off.
  3. Select cooling or heating mode, then use the temperature buttons to adjust.
  4. A comfortable setting is usually around 22 to 24°C.
  5. Please turn it off when you leave the house and keep doors and windows closed while it is running.

If the unit does not respond right away, point the remote at the indoor unit and try again.

Simple Oven, Microwave, And Fridge Instructions

  • Microwave: put food in a microwave-safe container, close the door, choose the time, and press start.
  • Do not use metal inside the microwave.
  • Oven: choose the cooking function, set the temperature, and allow a few minutes to preheat before cooking.
  • LG side-by-side fridge: one side is the fridge and the other is the freezer. Please make sure both doors are fully closed after use.
  • Dispenser: the second button from the right changes the dispenser from water to ice.
  • Drinking water: the fridge gives filtered water and it is recommended for drinking.
  • Please do not adjust the fridge or freezer temperature. It is already set for good performance.
  • When you finish, switch the oven and microwave off.

If anything seems unusual, please stop and send a WhatsApp message for help.
