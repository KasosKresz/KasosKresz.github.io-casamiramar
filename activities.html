Things to do in Salobreña

🏖 Beaches

Playa de Salobreña – Main beach with restaurants and beach bars.

Playa del Caletón – Smaller beach with beautiful castle views.

Playa de la Charca-Salomar.

🍽 Restaurants near Casa Miramar

La Botica – Great tapas in the old town.

El Peñón – Excellent seafood restaurant.

La Traviesa – Traditional Andalusian food.

🏛 Places to Visit

Salobreña Castle – Moorish castle with panoramic views.

Mirador Enrique Morente – One of the best viewpoints.

Old Town – Wander the white streets and discover cafés.

🛒 Shopping

Mercadona – Large supermarket.

Local fruit shops – Fresh mangoes and avocados.

Weekly market – Local produce and crafts.

🚗 Day Trips

Granada – Visit the Alhambra (1 hour).

Nerja – Famous caves and Balcón de Europa.

Las Alpujarras – Beautiful mountain villages.
