For owners who need full local support

Managing a property from another city or country is difficult when guests, permits, cleaners, trades, and reporting all need attention. This service is designed for owners who want their home operated professionally while still being cared for by people close to the property.

The focus is simple: keep the home clean, functional, compliant, welcoming, and ready for paid guest stays all year round.

Why work with a local manager

We support the local and hyper-local economy. Cleaning, maintenance, plumbing, electrical work, supplies, and services are sourced from Salobreña and the surrounding area whenever possible.

That means local people are hired for cleaning and maintenance, local shops and suppliers are preferred for purchases, and urgent problems can be handled by trades who know the area.
